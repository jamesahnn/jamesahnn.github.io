Hello!

Born in Seoul, South Korea and raised in Vancouver, Canada, I grew up as a curious individual seeking opportunities to portray my thoughts. I am currently enrolled in the program Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo and believe that this program has introduced me to many opportunities in the real world and a path where designing simply became a form of self-therapy.

I worked at a start-up called Tugolo and re-designed their whole website and mobile pages. I found the joy of designing and creating a new interface, knowing that many people will benefit from it. Tugolo was a special experience because I got to step my foot into the travel industry. I learned many valuable skills and wish to pursue this industry in the near future to satisfy my passion for travelling.

I am passionate about continuously learning more as a designer and strive to further grasp a deeper understanding in this industry.
