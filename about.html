about me

Hello...

Born in Seoul, South Korea and raised in Vancouver, Canada, I grew up as a curious individual seeking opportunities to portray my thoughts. I am currently enrolled in the program Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo. I believe that this program has introduced me to many opportunities in the real world and a path where designing simply became a form of self-therapy.

I worked at a start-up called Tugolo and re-designed their whole website and mobile pages. I found the joy of designing and creating a new interface, knowing that many people will benefit from it. Tugolo was a special experience because I got to step my foot into the travel industry. I learned so much and wish to pursue this industry in the near future again to satisfy my passion for travelling.

Following up with my peers, I am continously learning more as a designer and strive to further grasp a deeper understanding in this industry.

Thanks for listening to my Ted Talk and feel free to chat with me!
